Emergency Department

The Emergency Department at NewLife Hospital and Medical Center provides care to patients with urgent medical conditions ranging from complex problems such as heart attacks and strokes to simple but pressing conditions such as cuts and broken bones. Care is provided 24 hours a day, 7 days of the week and accepts patients of all ages, with all types of emergencies.

The Emergency Department is a 40-bed facility including a 4-bed Resuscitation Room, 4-bed Resuscitation Step Down Room with 2 ventilators (breathing machines), 16-bed Critical Care Area, 12-bed Observation Area, 8 Clinical Decision Units, 10-bed dedicated child (paediatric) Area, 1 Isolation Room, 1 Emergency Room, 2 Fast Track clinics and 3 Extended Triage beds. We provide emergency services to an average of 100 patients daily and ​more than 200-400 patients every day.

Urology Department

The Department of Urology at NewLife Hospital is one of the outstanding urology units of the city. We deal with the diseases of both male and female urinary tracts as well as conditions of the male reproductive organs. Our department has made great strides in the field of urology and offers a whole set of services comprising minimally-invasive and endoscopic removal of kidney stones, uro-oncology, paediatric urology, andrology, neuro-urology and uro-gynaecology.
Our team of well-trained doctors deals with any incoming urological polytraumas in the best possible way. We provide 24 hours round the clock emergency services in hospital for urological patients, note able emergencies are given below in service.



Cardiology Department

Department of Cardiology caters to a huge number of heart disease patients with simple to complex conditions on a daily basis. Such patients often have multiple co-morbid conditions and usually require multidisciplinary patient care. Since Liaquat National Hospital is a tertiary care hospital, the cardiac patients have the facility of being looked after by the consultants from various disciplines. Likewise, well trained cardiology consultants provide cardiac facilities to patients who are admitted in other medical and surgical disciplines. We have one of the largest private Coronary Care Unit in Karachi.
We have the most professional and dedicated staff for the most comprehensive cardiology treatment due to which we are ranked among the best in the country. We aim to provide our patients with the best services and treatments, to make them feel no less than a family member.
