About NLH

NewLife Hospital Founded in 2016, NewLife Hospital is located in Karachi, Pakistan. For more than 4 years we have been fulfilling the medical needs of our community. NewLife Hospital is continually improving the scope of services and skills available within the country. Our staff of Board certified doctors provide a full spectrum of treatment and preventative services.

Our nurses and support staff are committed to your wellbeing.Our services are designed to provide the most comprehensive benefits to patients from all walks of life.Since we opened our doors, close to 2 million people from all over Pakistan and abroad have come to NewLife Hospital for inpatient and outpatient treatment thanks to our excellence in health care
